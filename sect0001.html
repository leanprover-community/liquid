Introduction

The goal of this document is to provide a detailed account of the proof of the following theorem, along side a computer verification in the Lean theorem prover (see Section ??).

Theorem Clausen–Scholze
#

Let \(0 {\lt} p' {\lt} p \le 1\) be real numbers, let \(S\) be a profinite set, and let \(V\) be a \(p\)-Banach space. Let \(\mathcal M_{p'}(S)\) be the space of \(p'\)-measures on \(S\). Then

\[ \operatorname{Ext}^i_{\operatorname{Cond}(\operatorname{Ab})}(\mathcal M_{p'}(S), V)=0 \]

for \(i \ge 1\).

We will explain this statement in more detail in Section ?? below.

Remark
#
